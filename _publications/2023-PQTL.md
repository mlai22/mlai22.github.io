excerpt: "We integrated CSF proteomics with genetic data to investigate causal molecular mechanisms underlying familial Parkinson's disease. The statistical framework combined proteome-wide association analysis, pQTL mapping, Mendelian randomization, and colocalization analysis to prioritize proteins whose genetically regulated abundance is causally associated with PD. These integrative analyses identified candidate causal proteins and disease-associated pathways that may serve as therapeutic targets."
