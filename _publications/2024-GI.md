excerpt: "This study identified molecular markers associated with progressive gut dysfunction in Parkinson's disease by integrating clinical and multi-omics data. The statistical framework incorporated differential expression analysis, multivariable regression modeling, and FDR-adjusted multiple testing to identify robust associations between molecular features and self-reported gastrointestinal symptom progression. The results highlighted immune- and inflammation-related pathways that may contribute to gut dysfunction in PD."
