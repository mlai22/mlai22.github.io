excerpt: "The study identified distinct proteomic signatures across PD genetic subtypes and demonstrated that integrating proteomic and genomic data improves molecular stratification of Parkinson's disease. The statistical framework combined differential expression analysis for identifying proteomic signatures, weighted gene co-expression network analysis (WGCNA) for detecting protein modules associated with clinical and CSF biomarkers, and LASSO regression for biomarker selection and prediction. These analyses identified proteomic signatures that distinguish genetic PD subtypes and correlate with clinical and biomarker phenotypes."
